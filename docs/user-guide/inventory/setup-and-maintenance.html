Setup & Maintenance

The Setup & Maintenance menu enlists the following sub-menus:

Store Types There can be various types of stores based on the function of the store. For example: Retail store, Godown, Warehouse
Stores This is a place where you store your goods. For example: warehouse, shop, godown, etc.
Counter Setup A place in the shop where customers can pay money or cash. There can be many counters in one store. Create counters to allow only specific centers to trade with cash in your organization.
Party Types Parties can differ in their functionalities. A party can be a supplier or a customer or dealer.
Party Accounts Party is a person/entity that is involved in a transaction.
Shipping Addresses Shipping Address is the address where the products/ goods ordered by a party is shipped to.
Item Maintenance Items are products that are to be traded. Items can either be goods or services. Create items that are to be involved in the organization business.
Compound Items Compound Items refer to items formed using two or more items. For Eg: A computer can be a compound item created using keyboard, CPU and monitor.
Compound Item Details Compound Item Details specify the unit and quantity of item that the compound item. Additionaly, the price and discount of the compound item can also be configured.
Cost Prices Enter the cost price of the item. This cost price overrides the cost price of the item specified in Item Maintenance.
Selling Prices Enter the selling price of the item. This selling price overrides the selling price of the item specified in Item Maintenance.
Item Groups Item groups are the logical grouping of items. For example: Mobile phones can have all brands of mobile phones.
Item Types The type of item can be specified. Generally, used in manufacturing the item type can specify when to use the item. For example: Component type can specify the item as a component
Brands Create the brands that are available in your stores.
Units of Measure Units are standardized terms of measurable quantities. For Example: 1 Dozen = 12 pieces.
Compound Units of Measure Customized units can be created for compound units. A dozen can have 15 pieces in the compound item.
Shipper Information There can be multiple shipping agencies handling shipments. The detail information of the shippers can be included in this section.
